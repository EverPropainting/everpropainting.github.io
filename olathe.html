Why Olathe Homeowners Choose EverPro

Olathe is one of the fastest-growing cities in Kansas, and most of its housing stock was built in the same few decades — which means whole neighborhoods hit repaint age together. If your subdivision went up in the 90s or 2000s, odds are your hardboard siding is showing it: fading south walls, caulk pulling away at the seams, and bottom edges starting to swell.

That's exactly the kind of work we do every week. Hardboard and composite siding can last decades more — but only if failing spots are corrected before paint, not painted over. We scrape, sand, caulk every seam and nail hole with SherMAX, prime bare spots, and then paint — so the finish holds through Kansas summers.

  • Subdivision & HOA experience — we'll help you pick colors that clear your HOA and stand out anyway
  • Hardboard siding specialists — correct the failing edges instead of painting over them
  • Interior & cabinet work — golden-oak kitchens refinished at a fraction of replacement cost
