Living in your home while we work in it

Interior painting is personal — we're working inside your life, not just on your house. That shapes how every EverPro interior job runs:

  • Proper surface prep — nail holes filled, cracks repaired, surfaces sanded and spot-primed so the finish is smooth, not just colored

Interior Prep — Protecting Your Home Comes First

Before a single can is opened, your home gets locked down. This is the part of the job you'll never see in the finished photos, and it's the part that makes the difference between a clean project and paint where it doesn't belong:

  • Floors papered and taped — full coverage across every work area, seams taped down so nothing shifts underfoot or bleeds through
  • Rooms masked off — when we're spraying ceilings, entire rooms get sealed in plastic sheeting so overspray never travels
  • Furniture moved and covered — everything staying in the room is centered and wrapped
  • Fixtures, outlets, and hardware protected — taped or removed, never painted around
  • Surfaces made ready — nail holes filled, cracks repaired, walls sanded and spot-primed so the finish is smooth, not just colored

It's your home, not our job site — that's the standard, and it's why the only evidence we were ever there is the paint.

Interior Painting FAQs

Can we stay in the house during the job?

Almost always, yes. We work room by room so your home stays livable, and we'll plan the sequence around your family's routine during the walkthrough.

How long does interior painting take?

A few rooms typically take a couple of days; whole-home repaints run longer. Your written quote includes a clear timeline before we start.

Do you help with color selection?

Yes — Ethan walks through color and sheen choices with you during the estimate, including what holds up best in high-traffic areas, kids' rooms, bathrooms, and kitchens.

What does interior painting cost?

It depends on the rooms, ceilings, trim, and surface condition — not just square footage. Read how we price every job, then get your real number with a free walkthrough. The number we quote is the number you pay.
