Why Mission Homeowners Choose EverPro

Mission and its northeast Johnson County neighbors — Roeland Park, Fairway, Merriam, Prairie Village — are full of post-war ranches and Cape Cods with a signature look: solid brick paired with wood trim, soffits, and siding accents. Those homes are trim-heavy jobs, and trim is where cheap paint work fails first.

Mature trees make these some of the prettiest streets in the metro — and some of the most shaded, which means north faces and soffits collect mildew and hold moisture. Our prep addresses that directly: wash, treat, scrape, sand, prime, caulk — then paint. The result is crisp trim lines against that classic brick that hold up for years.

  • Post-war home experience — trim, soffits, and siding accents done right against brick
  • Mildew & shade-side prep — treated and primed, not painted over
  • Interior refreshes — smaller rooms, bigger trim detail; exactly the work we love

Mission Painting FAQs

Do you work in Mission and the surrounding NE Johnson County cities?

Yes — Mission, Roeland Park, Fairway, Westwood, Merriam, and Prairie Village are all in our regular rotation, minutes from our Overland Park base.

My wood trim is peeling but the brick is fine. Do you do partial exteriors?

All the time. Brick-and-trim homes are often trim-scope jobs — we'll quote exactly what your home needs and nothing it doesn't.

How do you handle mildew on shaded siding?

We wash and treat it before any paint goes on, then prime with the right product. Painting over mildew just seals the problem in — that's the shortcut we don't take.

What does painting cost in Mission?

It depends on scope and surface condition — trim-heavy homes are priced on detail work, not square footage. Free written estimates, and the number holds.
