Why Kansas City Homeowners Choose EverPro

Kansas City proper — both sides of the state line — has the metro's most varied housing stock: 1920s bungalows and shirtwaists in Brookside and Waldo, mid-century ranches across KCK and the Northland, and newer builds filling in everywhere between. No two blocks paint the same, and that's exactly why experience matters.

Older KC homes are wood-heavy and prep-intensive: original siding, trim, and porches that need scraping, sanding, priming, and caulk before they'll hold paint. Newer homes need the builder shortcuts corrected. Every home gets a walkthrough with Ethan, a scope built for what it actually needs, and Sherwin-Williams products matched to the surface.

  • Older-home expertise — bungalows, shirtwaists, and four-squares done right
  • Both sides of the state line — KCMO and KCK
  • Full scope — exterior, interior, and cabinet refinishing

Kansas City Painting FAQs

Do you paint inside the city or just the suburbs?

Both — we work across Kansas City proper on both the Missouri and Kansas sides, plus the full suburban metro.

My house is 100 years old. Can you handle it?

Yes — older homes are prep-heavy, detail-heavy work, which is our specialty. We'll be honest about what the wood needs and what's worth doing.

What does house painting cost in Kansas City?

It depends on the home more than the neighborhood — size, stories, and surface condition. Free walkthrough, written quote, and the number holds.

Do you handle lead-safe practices on older homes?

We follow safe work practices appropriate to each home's age and condition, and we'll discuss the specifics for your house during the walkthrough.
