About EverPro Painting

A family business built on a simple idea: paint every home like it's our own.

Meet Ethan

EverPro Painting is owned and operated by Ethan Sageser, based right here in Overland Park. When you call, Ethan answers. When you get an estimate, Ethan walks your home with you. And when the crew is on your house, Ethan is checking the work while it happens — not after.

This is a family business in the most literal sense — built to support his wife and young son, which is exactly why every job carries his name and his standard. There's no sales department, no rotating project managers, and no gap between the person who made you a promise and the person responsible for keeping it.

That structure isn't an accident. It's the whole point.

We Live Here

EverPro isn't a big chain with a Kansas City branch — and it isn't a franchise running crews out of a call center three states away. We live here. Our son will grow up here. The homes we paint are on the streets we drive every day, in the neighborhoods where we shop, eat, and cheer on the Chiefs.

That changes how we work. When your painter is your neighbor, the job isn't done when the check clears — it's done when we'd be proud to drive past your house for the next ten years. Serving this community isn't a marketing line for us; it's the whole reason the company exists.

So when you hire EverPro, your money stays in Kansas City, your painter answers his own phone, and the name on the yard sign is a family that lives ten minutes away.

What We Believe

Prep Is the Job

Anyone can make a house look good on day one. We're after year ten — which is why we scrape, sand, caulk, and prime before a drop of finish paint goes on. The work you can't see is the work that lasts.

Honesty Over the Sale

If something doesn't need paint, we'll tell you. If it needs repair before paint, we'll tell you that too. The number we quote is the number you pay — and we'd rather lose a bid than earn one with a lowball that grows later.

Your Home, Our Name

Floors papered, furniture covered, job sites cleaned every day. We work in your home the way we'd want a crew working in ours — because every finished job around KC carries the EverPro name on it.

Proudly Serving the Kansas City Metro

Based in Overland Park and working across Johnson County and the metro — Olathe, Lenexa, Shawnee, Leawood, Prairie Village, Lee's Summit, Blue Springs, Liberty, and beyond. Rated ★★★★★ by homeowners on Google, painting with Sherwin-Williams products, licensed and insured.
