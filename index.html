Frequently Asked Questions

How much does it cost to paint a house in Kansas City?

Honest answer: it depends on your home — size, stories, surface condition, and most of all how much prep the job needs to actually last. Anyone who quotes a price without walking your home is guessing with your money. Ethan walks every home personally, goes through exactly what it needs with you, and you get a detailed written quote — the number we quote is the number you pay, start to finish. Read how we price every job.

How long will my paint job last?

An exterior paint job lasts 8–15 years in Kansas City's climate. Where your home lands in that range comes down to two things: the prep and the paint. Prep is our standard on every job — scrape, sand, caulk, prime, no shortcuts. The paint is where you have options, and the quality you choose is what pushes a job toward that 15-year mark. We'll go over those products in detail during your estimate and find what's best for your home.

Are you licensed and insured?
