Why Blue Springs Homeowners Choose EverPro

Blue Springs neighborhoods from the 80s, 90s, and 2000s share a story: open lots, strong sun exposure, and hardboard or vinyl-and-trim exteriors that fade and fail at the caulk lines first. South- and west-facing walls take the worst of it, which is why half-faded homes are such a common sight.

Our answer is a prep-first repaint with products rated for exactly this: seams re-caulked with SherMAX, failing spots scraped, sanded, and primed, and finish coats from Sherwin-Williams lines built to hold color through UV and freeze-thaw. The dramatic color flips we've done on homes like these are some of our proudest work.

  • Whole-home color changes — dated palettes made modern
